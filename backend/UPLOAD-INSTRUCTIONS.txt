# FILES TO UPLOAD TO CPANEL

## Upload these folders (with all their contents):
1. routes/
2. config/
3. middleware/
4. utils/

## Upload these individual files:
1. server-production.js  (RENAME to server.js after upload)
2. database.js
3. package.json
4. .htaccess

- node_modules/
- .env files
- .db or .sqlite files
- Any migration scripts

1. Rename server-production.js to server.js
2. Continue with Node.js App setup
